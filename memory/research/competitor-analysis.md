# Competitor Analysis: MeliOps
Date: 2026-05-11

## Executive Summary
MeliOps competes against generic PDF tools (poor UX for this specific task) and Chrome extensions (often buggy/insecure). High opportunity to position as the secure, cloud-native standard.

## 1. Generic PDF Tools (iLovePDF, Smallpdf)
- **Strengths**: High domain authority, massive traffic.
- **Weaknesses**: They merge PDFs but DO NOT crop bounding boxes or handle the 2x2/2x3 grid specific to Mercado Libre. Manual effort required by user.
- **Our Edge**: "1-click optimization tailored for Mercado Libre, no manual cropping."

## 2. Chrome Extensions
- **Strengths**: Integrated directly into browser, quick access.
- **Weaknesses**: Break frequently when ML updates DOM, security/privacy concerns, require installation.
- **Our Edge**: "No installation needed. Secure login. Works on any device."

## 3. High-End ERPs (Multivende, Envíopack)
- **Strengths**: End-to-end logistics.
- **Weaknesses**: Overkill and expensive for small/medium sellers who just want to print labels.
- **Our Edge**: "Free tier, focused solely on the printing problem, affordable Pro plans."

## Positioning (Battlecard)
- **Who we are**: The fastest, safest cloud tool to group ML labels.
- **Why us over iLovePDF**: We auto-crop the label bounding box.
- **Why us over Extensions**: No malware risk, no installation, works on mobile.
